    Tips for Effective Focus

    • Start small: Begin with 25-minute focus sessions and gradually increase as you build your focus muscle.
    • Block digital distractions: Add websites that typically distract you to the block list.
    • Create a physical distraction-free environment: Clear your desk, silence your phone, and let others know you're in a focus session.
    • Take proper breaks: After your focus session, take a 5-minute break to rest your mind.
    • Stay hydrated: Keep water nearby to maintain optimal brain function.
